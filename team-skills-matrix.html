Team Skills Matrix for Computer Enterprises, Inc.

I worked on this project over the span of 6 weeks during my remote internship at Computer Enterprises, Inc. (CEI) during the summer of 2020. The aim of the project was to create a website that would allow employees at CEI to search a registry of other employees by their skills, certifications, industries of expertise, location, and employment type. Employees would be able to both create their own profile and browse others’ profiles. I did UX along with an intern with a digital design background and development with an intern with a computer science background.

Prototypes

Though the project wasn’t completed by the time I left, I learned and grew so much. It was my first time really using version control software or working on any software project for so long, and I had never even heard of Angular or TypeScript when I started. By the end of my time at CEI, I felt confident in these technologies, and I would love to get the chance to use them again.

If I could go back or work on this more, I would add some of the features we had to cut for the MVP, spend more time talking to the stakeholder, and spend less time trying to perfect parts of early iterations that ended up being changed later.
